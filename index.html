About JAG

JAG (Just Another Guardian at Place) is an intelligent cybersecurity platform designed to proactively detect, analyze, and respond to cyber threats in real time.

By combining artificial intelligence, behavioral analytics, and continuous monitoring, JAG helps organizations defend against modern attacks before damage occurs.

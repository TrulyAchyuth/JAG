About JAG

JAG JAG (Just Another Guardian at Place) is an advanced cybersecurity platform designed to monitor, detect, and respond to cyber threats in real time. Built to simulate real-world Security Operations Center (SOC) environments, JAG combines AI-driven behavioral analysis, continuous system monitoring, and centralized log management to deliver intelligent and proactive threat detection. The platform provides automated alerts, incident prioritization, and rapid response capabilities to minimize risk and reduce attack impact. With support for cloud, endpoint, and on-premise infrastructures, JAG offers scalable, enterprise-grade security architecture that ensures complete visibility, faster threat mitigation, and reliable protection against modern cyber threats.


  • ✔ AI-driven behavioral threat detection
  • ✔ Real-time monitoring of systems and networks
  • ✔ Centralized log collection and analysis
  • ✔ SOC-ready enterprise-grade architecture
  • ✔ Cloud, endpoint & on-premise security coverage
